The Atelier Story

From a single bean to a daily ritual.

Our Origins

Rooted in Quality

Founded in 2020, Espresso Atelier began with a simple philosophy: coffee is not just a beverage, it is a craft. We traveled to the highlands of Ethiopia and the mountains of Colombia to build direct relationships with farmers who share our obsession with quality.

Every batch is roasted in-house, ensuring that the unique notes of each origin—be it floral, nutty, or chocolatey—are preserved and celebrated.

The Space

Designed for You

Our cafe is more than a place to get caffeine. It is a sanctuary. Designed by award-winning architects, our space blends industrial raw materials with warm, inviting textures.

Whether you are here to work, catch up with an old friend, or simply watch the world go by, Espresso Atelier is your canvas.
